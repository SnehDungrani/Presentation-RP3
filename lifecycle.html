Resilience · deep dive

Every station shares one heartbeat

Arm or shuttle or belt - under the hood, every station is the same kind of object: a Module with five life stages. That uniformity is why the simulator can fail and recover any piece without special cases.

idle → busy → draining ⤳ failed → recovering → idle
⚠

Detect

Backend pings every container every 2 s. Three missed beats and the station is marked failed; downstream stations halt automatically.

⏱

Grace window

Recovery isn't instant. A two-second timer holds the station in recovering while the container settles, so the UI never lies about readiness.

↺

Resume in place

Once the container reports idle, the line keeps moving. Any in-flight part is re-queued, not dropped - race-tested.
